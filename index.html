About Me

My Journey

Hi, my name is Alejandro, I am passionate creator dedicated to exploring the boundaries of visual expression. My journey began over 8 years ago when I first discovered my love for mixed media and digital art through drawing, painting, and photography.

My Approach

I believe that art should challenge perspectives and help create meaningful connections with others. Each piece I create is an exploration of color, texture, and emotion.
